About me

My name is Brian Flieck. I am the Director of Retouching and Workflow Systems at Industrial Color Studios. I have worked in the E-commerce studio enviroment for the past 20 years. I started my carrer working for Global Sports owned by Micheal Rubin as an intern. Soon after college I gained full time employement with Global Sports. Later the company name would change to GSI commerce, then Ebay Enterprise, and now Industrial Color.


I have spent several years learning basic automation in PERL to help move files around, validate images, create logs, archive images. I'm completely self taught and wanted to expand my knownegle base to include full stack web development.
